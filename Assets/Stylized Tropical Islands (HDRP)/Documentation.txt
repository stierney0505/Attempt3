Stylized Tropical Islands (HDRP)

This package works only in HDRP, Unity version 2020.2.2f1 and higher.

Stylized tropical vegetation with a custom shader that helps to control random HUE variation.
Water shader with verteex displacement, fade-out near the shore, and 2 scrolling normal maps.
Simple rock, and rock_cliff shaders with detail normals and material blending with custom masks.

Project includes (37) high quality, well optimized assets, created for AAA quality visuals.
High quality textures - 2048x2048 pixels (Albedo, Mask, Normal map and Thickness map for vegetation)

All of the vegetation besides trees are combined into one texture, 
and all rocks use one material to reduse draw calls.

Triangle count depends on complexity of the mesh, highest one being 16637 triangles.
Most of the models have LODs. (4 avg.)

RGB mask for cliffs
R - edges
G - mask for moss (grass)
B - top-down gradient
